Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2025

At LTU Health Analytics, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information.

1. Data Collection & Purpose

We collect personal data (Name, Age, NHS Number) and medical metrics (BMI, Glucose, etc.) solely for the purpose of Stroke Risk Estimation. This data is processed under GDPR Article 9(2)(h) (Provision of Health/Social Care).

2. Data Security

  • All Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is Encrypted at Rest using AES-128 (Fernet).
  • Data is stored in a secure Hybrid Database architecture.
  • Access is restricted via Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).

3. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data via the Patient Dashboard.
  • Rectify inaccurate data via the "Update Profile" feature.
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") by contacting the System Administrator.
