About Our Initiative

Bridging the gap between advanced medical technology and secure, compassionate patient care.

Our Mission

The LTU Health Department Project was founded with a singular vision: to reduce the global impact of stroke through early detection and secure data monitoring.

We leverage cutting-edge data science to track critical risk factors—such as hypertension, glucose levels, and BMI—allowing for timely medical intervention. Our platform is designed not just for doctors, but for patients, empowering individuals to take ownership of their health journey.

Security & Ethics

As part of the COM7033 Secure Software Development module, we prioritize data privacy above all else. We adhere to strict SSDLC (Secure Software Development Life Cycle) principles, ensuring that all patient records are encrypted, authenticated, and handled in full compliance with GDPR standards.
